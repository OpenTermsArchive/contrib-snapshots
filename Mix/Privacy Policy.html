Does Mix Use Cookies or Other Tracking Methods?

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies for strictly necessary functions to make the Site work and to provide the personalized and curated feed you request from us when you set up an account and, depending on your choices and applicable law, for analytics, personalization, and advertising. Depending on where you are accessing the services from you may see a banner requesting consent for certain tracking when you first land on the Site. Wherever you are accessing the services from you can manage your choices at any time by clicking on “Cookie Settings” at the top of this page, in the hamburger menu.

  • Cookies. Cookies are small text files placed in visitors' computer browsers to store their preferences. Most browsers allow you to block and delete cookies and depending on where you are accessing the Services from, you may be able to choose which cookies are set. However, if you disable or remove some cookies, some areas of the Site may not work properly and we may not be able to provide the Services.
  • Pixel Tags/Web Beacons. A pixel tag (also known as a web beacon) is a piece of code embedded on the Site that collects information about users' engagement on that web page. The use of a pixel allows us to record, for example, that a user has visited a particular web page or clicked on a particular advertisement.
  • Social Media. Our Site includes social media features which allow you to create an account or access your account or to see certain content using your own social media accounts. These features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our Site, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly, such as to access your account or in order to play a video. These social media features are either hosted by a Third Party or hosted directly on our Site. Your information will be collected by these third parties when you engage with certain content features and content. We also work with social media companies to help deliver our ads to you online. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it such as those listed above.

The online advertising industry also provides websites from which you may opt out of receiving targeted ads from data partners and other advertising partners that participate in self-regulatory programs. You can access these and learn more about targeted advertising and consumer choice and privacy by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative, the Digital Advertising Alliance, the European Digital Advertising Alliance, and the Digital Advertising Alliance of Canada. Please note to take advantage of these opt-outs, you must opt out separately on each browser and on each device.

Our uses of such Technologies fall into the following general categories:

  • Strictly Necessary. We may use Technologies that are necessary to the operation of our Site, Services, applications, and tools. This includes technologies that allow you access to our Site, Services, applications, and tools; that are required to, prevent or detect fraudulent activity and technical faults, or that allow you to make use of our functions such as authenticating a User to deliver the personalized and curated feed which you request from us when you create a Mix account or similar functions.
  • Functionality. We may use cookies, web beacons, or other similar technologies that offer enhanced functionality and preference selection when accessing or using our Site, Services, applications, or tools. This may include keeping track of your specified preferences such as language or region.
  • Analytics & Performance. We may use cookies, web beacons, or other similar technologies to assess the performance of our Site, Services, applications, and tools, including as part of our analytic practices to help us understand how our visitors use our Sites, determine if you have interacted with our messaging, determine whether you have viewed an item or link, or to improve our Site content or identifying irregular Site activity and improve security, Services, applications, or tools.
