Navigator Introduction

The Navigator app provides turn-by-turn navigation with traffic-based routing and three-dimensional city views. Navigator privacy practices are covered by Verizon’s Privacy Policy as well as the practices described here. If there is a conflict between the two policies, the practices described in this policy govern when you are using Navigator.

Read Verizon’s Privacy Policy

 

Information we collect and how it is used

We collect and use location information, mobile telephone number and mobile device identifiers, information about your travel and speed, your use of Navigator services and your contacts (when you request navigation to a contact’s address).

This information is used to deliver, maintain and support the service. to enhance application features. and to customize and personalize your experiences in the Navigator app.

Information we share.

We share de-identified information about your location and travel speed with third-parties that use it to enhance the traffic reporting capabilities of this application.

Your choices.

You can use your device location settings to change your choice about whether Navigator uses location information.
