What This Privacy Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy describes the information Comcast collects about you (“Customer Information”) when you subscribe to and use the Xfinity Mobile service and any Xfinity Mobile applications used to access information about your mobile account (collectively, “Xfinity Mobile Service”). This Privacy Policy also explains how Comcast uses and shares that information, how Comcast protects your privacy when you use the Xfinity Mobile Service, and how you may exercise choices regarding the use and sharing of such information.

What This Privacy Policy Does Not Cover

This Privacy Policy does not cover other Comcast services (such as XFINITY TV, XFINITY Voice, XFINITY Internet, or XFINITY Home), websites (such as xfinity.com and xfinitymobile.com), or Comcast apps (such as Xfinity Stream or Xfinity My Account). These services have their own privacy policies, which we post at http://www.xfinity.com/customerprivacy/ and http://my.xfinity.com/privacy/, respectively.

This Privacy Policy also does not apply to any other apps that you install on your mobile device. Those apps may collect information about you or your device, such as your contacts, usage, or location. You should read the privacy policies associated with any apps you install on your mobile device.

The Information Comcast Collects for Xfinity Mobile Service and How It Is Used and Shared

This Privacy Policy applies to the account owner who purchases Xfinity Mobile Service and any user of Xfinity Mobile Service on or through your account. In this Privacy Policy, “you” means the account owner and all of these users.

We want you to understand what kinds of information we collect when you subscribe to and use Xfinity Mobile Service. how Comcast uses, shares, and protects that information. the choices you have regarding its use and sharing. and how you may exercise those choices.

Comcast collects the following types of Customer Information from people who use the Xfinity Mobile Service:

Information We Collect When You Create an Account

We collect information from you to create an account with us. This includes payment information such as your payment card or bank account information or information related to a credit application for Xfinity Mobile Service, which may include your Social Security number or other identification, such as a driver’s license number. If you subscribe to XFINITY Internet, we access and use existing XFINITY account information to help create your Xfinity Mobile Service account.

Information We Collect When You Use the Xfinity Mobile Service

Comcast and third parties acting on Comcast’s behalf collect technical and service information from all Xfinity Mobile Service users, which we call “Usage Data.” Usage Data includes app usage information, equipment information, network performance and usage information, and location information. This includes information about your use of the Xfinity Mobile network, use of your device, and diagnostic data such as device performance, signal strength, dropped calls, data failures, battery strength, and network performance issues. This may also include information about what apps are on your device, the fact that an app has been used, and the length of time that an app has been running. Other information includes voice recordings or prints, reasons you give for contacting us, network traffic data, device identifiers, service options, and the number of devices you have purchased on our plans.

Location Information

This is any information from your device that tells us where your device is located at a specific point in time.

How Customer Information Is Used and SharedCustomer Information May be Shared Among Comcast Affiliates

If you use Xfinity Mobile Service in connection with other Xfinity services or apps (for example, when you access Xfinity Wi-Fi with your Xfinity Mobile device or watch videos using Xfinity Stream, we may share your Customer Information with the Comcast companies, including subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively, “Comcast Companies”) that offer those services. We provide this information so that these Comcast Companies can provide services to you and to make it easier for you to use Xfinity Mobile Service and other Xfinity services, like Xfinity Stream, seamlessly.

Customer Information May be Shared With Third Parties That Help Us Provide Xfinity Mobile Service to You

Comcast uses third parties to assist us in delivering parts of the Xfinity Mobile Service to you.

We may share Customer Information with these third parties to the extent necessary for them to provide these services. These companies are acting on Comcast's behalf and are required, by contract with Comcast, to keep this information confidential and are only authorized to use it for specific purposes.

Sharing and Disclosure of Customer Information for Other Reasons

We respect and protect your privacy, but we may be required to provide information about you to a court of law or a law enforcement agency upon the receipt of valid legal process, such as a subpoena, court order, or search warrant. We may also disclose Customer Information to protect our rights or the rights of others and in emergency situations.

If you are not the primary Xfinity Mobile Service account owner, you should know that the primary account owner may have access to Customer Information about you and your use of Xfinity Mobile Service.

If Comcast's business changes—for example, if our company merges with another company or we sell one or more of the Comcast companies that provide Xfinity Mobile Service—we may give the new owners Customer Information in anticipation of or after the completion of any transaction. If there is a change of ownership, we will tell you using any means permitted by law, including via e-mail and/or a prominent notice on xfinitymobile.com or on Xfinity Mobile My Account, as well as let you know of any choices you may have regarding your Customer Information.

Use and Sharing of De-Identified or Aggregate Information

We may share de-identified or aggregate data with third parties who commit not to re-identify such data.

Your Choices

You have choices with respect to the information we collect, use, and share. Read the Full Xfinity Mobile Customer Privacy Policy to learn about all of your choices. We will also let you know if this Privacy Policy changes, and what your choices are with respect to these changes. If you deny or restrict your consent for us to use your Customer Information in accordance with the choices available to you, you will experience no material effect, now or in the future, on how we provide the Xfinity Mobile Service to which you subscribe.

When you use the Xfinity Mobile Service, you are agreeing that this Xfinity Mobile Customer Privacy Policy applies to you.

Customer Information Subject to Additional Protection Under Federal Law

When we collect certain information about your use of the Xfinity Mobile Service, additional protections apply under United States telecommunications law and are reflected in this Privacy Policy, including protections for Customer Proprietary Network Information (“CPNI”). CPNI is defined in Section 222 of the Communications Act of 1934, as amended, and includes, but is not limited to, the phone numbers that you call and that call you, the times and dates of those calls, location information, billing and payment history, and service information and rate and data plans you use, and the number of devices connected to your Xfinity Mobile Service. Those additional protections and the choices you have regarding the use and sharing of such information are reflected in this Privacy Policy.

  • If you are an XFINITY Internet subscriber and tell us that you want to purchase Xfinity Mobile Service, we access and use your existing XFINITY account information to help create an account for the Xfinity Mobile Service.

Comcast and third parties acting on Comcast's behalf collect technical and service information from all Xfinity Mobile Service users, which we call "Usage Data." Usage Data includes:

  • App Usage Information. Xfinity Mobile Service apps covered by this Privacy Policy may allow you to store certain details and preferences ("Preference Data"). We do this so that you do not have to enter this data every time you return to an Xfinity Mobile Service app.

    • We may also use the Preference Data to deliver other personalized services and features of the Xfinity Mobile Service My Account app.

  • Equipment Information. This includes device IDs, device types, device status, device serial numbers, and information about the settings, configurations, and software on your device.
  • Network Performance and Usage Information.   • Location Information. This includes any information from your device that indicates where your device is located at a specific point in time. Location information is available when your device communicates with cell towers, Wi-Fi routers, access points, and/or with technologies used by the Global Positioning System.

    • Note: Other companies, like the manufacturer of your mobile device, the provider of your cellular connection, the provider of the operating system on your device, and providers of mobile applications installed on your device, may also be able to access and collect this information. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the location and other information collected by those manufacturers and providers.

  • Xfinity Mobile Service Apps. Xfinity Mobile Service apps covered by this Privacy Policy may collect location information, contact information, your phone number and the phone numbers of calls you make and receive, the device ID and device serial number from your device, and other information. For example, the Visual Voice Mail app will access your device's microphone and contact information from your device. The Xfinity Mobile Service My Account app will collect payment data from your Xfinity Mobile Service account and will collect data from your device about usage of the app.

3. Use of Customer Information

Comcast uses Customer Information in both de-identified and aggregated forms to provide and support the Xfinity Mobile Service. We process and use this data for purposes consistent with this Privacy Policy including, but not limited to:

  • Providing you with Xfinity Mobile Service by routing your calls, messages, and Internet traffic;
  • Analyzing, customizing, measuring, and improving the Xfinity Mobile Service;
  • Providing customer and technical support;
  • Providing 911 services;
  • Securing the Xfinity Mobile Service and our network;
  • Enforcing our Xfinity Mobile Customer Agreements and to detect fraud, illegal activities, and violations of our terms of service and acceptable use policies;
  • Assessing credit and payment risk;
  • Billing and collecting for any applicable fees or charges;
  • Providing you service-related communications about the Xfinity Mobile Service;
  • Letting you know about new products or services from Comcast or other companies we work with, as well as promotional offers, when we have any required consent to do so;
  • Letting you know about changes to the Xfinity Mobile Service, the Xfinity Mobile Customer Agreement, this Privacy Policy, and other terms that apply to our services. and
  • Updating any programs or other tools that we provide in conjunction with the Xfinity Mobile Service.

Comcast may use Customer Information for internal business purposes, such as to measure and analyze use of the Xfinity Mobile network and Internet traffic related to your Xfinity Mobile Service usage in Wi-Fi mode, as well as for supplier reporting and assessment of service levels, and to better understand how the Xfinity Mobile Service is used. We may also use Customer Information to create audience segments that are based on particular interests and/or characteristics shared by those in the segment. We may use that information to provide advertisements and promotional opportunities to you, when we have any required consent to do so.

We may also use location information to provide you with a customized experience, when we have any required consent to do so. For example, if we provide 411 Directory Assistance, we might use your wireless location information to provide you the number of the business location closest to you.

You may review and update your Customer Information by accessing Xfinity Mobile My Account online. If you need our help to correct or remove information, please contact us. We will respond to your request within 30 days. In some cases, we may not be able to correct or remove Customer Information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

If you make an affirmative, written request for a copy of your CPNI, we will disclose the relevant information we have to you at your account address of record, or to any person authorized by you, if we reasonably believe the request is valid. However, subscribers to Xfinity Mobile Service should be aware that we generally do not provide them with records of any inbound or outbound calls or other records that we don't furnish in the ordinary course of business (for example, as part of a bill) or which are available only from our archives, without valid legal process such as a court order.

We will retain Customer Information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Xfinity Mobile Service, and as otherwise necessary to help us improve our products and services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements with you.

When you have "Advanced Wi-Fi Settings" in your device turned on, Comcast uses your location to locate an Xfinity Wi-Fi access point near you and automatically connect your device to the Internet using Xfinity Wi-Fi. You may turn this function off in the "Advanced Wi-Fi Settings" in your device.

14. Special Note about XFINITY Wi-Fi

When your device is connected to the Xfinity Wi-Fi access point, the information collected and used is governed by the Comcast Customer Privacy Notice, located at http://www.xfinity.com/customerprivacy/. In addition to the information collection and use practices described in this Privacy Policy, Comcast may record information about your use of the WiFi Service for purposes of improving the efficiency and effectiveness of the WiFi Service and to automate repeated logins to the WiFi Service from the same device.
